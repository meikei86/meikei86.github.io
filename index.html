Our Vision

Now, towards the new era of manufacturing!

Now, the manufacturing world is welcoming an era of electronics information war. The main keyword is DATA. KMC is familair with Japanese manufacturing industry and creates a Japanese version of IoT M2M system that goes perfectly with actual manufacturing sites and supports a wide range of manufacturing innovation from small and medium enterprises to big enterprises.

KMC Solutions (M) Sdn. Bhd. as a subsidiary of KMC, holds the same vision of providing the state of the art technology developed by KMC. Our main products are M2M and ioT which are implemented in a number of industries in Japan. The M2M product group, which connects the production machines in the factory with a network, creates visibility for operational status. The IoT product group, which stores a huge amount of data such as knowledge of technicians and manufacturing process on the cloud, creates a database.

Our Smart Engineering concept holds these M2M and IoT together by our elite development team with rich experiences in the manufacturing industry.
